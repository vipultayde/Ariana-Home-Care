Our Method

The Ariana 4-Level Care Scale

Every patient undergoes a structured nursing assessment and is assigned a care plan matched to their condition — so you never pay for more than you need, and never receive less than you should.

Level 1

Basic Support

Companionship, supervision and help with daily activities for largely independent patients.

Level 2

Assisted Care

Trained attendant support for mobility, hygiene, feeding and routine health monitoring.

Level 3

Skilled Nursing

Qualified nursing procedures — wound care, injections, catheter and tube feeding management.

Level 4

Intensive / Palliative

Advanced clinical care for critical, post-ICU and palliative patients with close supervision.

The exact level is decided after a structured nursing assessment by our clinical team.
